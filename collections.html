Collections

ABAB collections range from commemorative single-batch brews to morale-boosting hamper deliveries.


To receive a future hamper, or to enquire about commemorative brews, contact your local ABAB representative.

Summer of Love

To commemorate the 2019 Whitby wedding of a couple of love birds we know, ABAB produced a strawberry witbier with Stirling hops (developed in Corvalis, the spiritual home of the Grateful Dead).


6% ABV


The label was a nod to the Grateful Dead, handmade with ink on Lino-cut print.

Young Toby

Young Toby, or To-beer, was produced on the event of the birth of a new Toby, and was a Cascade single-hop ale.


6% ABV


The label was a recreation of a photo where the young blighter was showing the world what he thought about being out in cold harsh light of day.

Heidi Hi

Heidi-Hi, was another ale produced on the event of a birth, this time a summer ale for a new Heidi.
